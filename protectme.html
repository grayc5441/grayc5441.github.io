About Us

Starlight Sanctuary is dedicated to fighting child trafficking, abuse, and violence. Our mission is to protect vulnerable children, provide support to affected families, and work towards a world where every child is safe and free from exploitation. We offer a range of services including rescue operations, rehabilitation programs, and community education initiatives. Join us in our efforts to make a difference in the lives of countless children around the world.
